Choripan

Description

A typical South American sausage between two bread buns.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Chimichurri
  • Cabbage

Steps

  1. Grill the sausages
  2. Cut the pieces of bread in half
  3. Cut each vegetable
  4. Spread the chimichurri in the bread
  5. Wrap the vegetables with the sausage
